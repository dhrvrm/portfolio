# SEO Marketing Analyst

## Instructions
- Identify target queries by intent and page relevance.
- Optimize on-page structure: titles, headings, internal links.
- Flag technical issues: crawlability, performance, schema.
- Propose measurable improvements and tracking suggestions.

## Deliverables
- Target keyword and intent mapping
- On-page recommendations
- Technical SEO checklist items

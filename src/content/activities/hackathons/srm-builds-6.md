First hackathon as a hacker to first time mentoring (for free, just helping out) in one week. The energy at SRM Builds 6.0 was electric. Code flying, music jamming, ideas colliding.

**Mentee teams crushed it.** 1st place plus several qualifying for finals. Late-night debugging sessions and impromptu concert breaks. That's hackathon magic.

Watched teams build, fail, pivot, and create something meaningful in hours.

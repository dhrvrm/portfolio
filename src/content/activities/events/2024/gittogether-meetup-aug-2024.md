Just walked out of the **GitTogether** Meetup with a brain full of code and a pocket full of ideas.

**Highlights from this tech feast:**

- **Unnati Chhabra** (Zomato) demystified their AI magic. Apparently, their chatbots aren't powered by caffeinated elves after all.
- **Shivay Lamba** (Couchbase) turned database testing from a snooze-fest into a thriller. Who knew deadlocks could be so riveting?
- **Karan M.V.** unveiled GitHub's master plan to become the Hogwarts of coding. GitHub Universe 2024, here we come!

Major props to **Chhavi Garg** and **Vipul Gupta** for crafting an atmosphere where "chill" meets "mind-blown". The group activity "from ideation to presentation" was a banger idea!

Met some amazing and experienced people like **Aman Singh**, **Tanmay Mohan**, **Prateek Saraswat**, **Ojasvi Bhargava**, **Kartik Gupta**, **Yogendra Singh**, **Om Mishra** and many great minds.

Left the developers meetup inspired. Next meetup can't come soon enough.

CityJS Conference Delhi. International speakers, great value and venue. Sainath Ramanathan on micro-frontends (architecture, scaling, real-world use). Shruti Balasa on scroll-driven animations in CSS, beyond GSAP, compositor thread and demos. Tapas Adhikary on scaling your front-end in a realistic way (tapaScript). Harshil Agrawal (Cloudflare) on WebSockets, real-time communication and OpenNext. Li Hau Tan live-coded Svelte 5 in under 30 minutes. Organised by CityJS Conferences, Vonage, Auth0, ImageKit.io. Communities behind it: JSLovers, React Delhi, WeMakeDevs. Went with Swastik Baranwal, Oshaib Beg, Vansh Kapoor, Jaskeerat Singh, Affan Ahmad. Met Bala Bhadra Maharjan, Ashutosh Shrestha, Vinay Shrestha, Deepesh Gupta, Ayush Kumar Tiwari, Mohd Ahmad, Subhankar Maiti, Subhang Singh Gaharwar, Shrey Sinha, Sahaj Singh.

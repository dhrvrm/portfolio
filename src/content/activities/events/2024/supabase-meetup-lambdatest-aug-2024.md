Supabase meetup at LambdaTest, Noida. Paul Copplestone and Ant Wilson introduced supabase.new. Rachit Khurana covered RAG, generative AI and vector embeddings with a live demo on image RAG using Supabase. Vansh Kapoor showed real-time streams: a chat app and collaborative whiteboard. Ish Kapoor on building VS Code extensions. Aarush Acharya closed with an intro to Edge Functions. Met Krish Khattar, Manik Rana, Swastik Baranwal, Vibha Pathak, Devansh Singh Parmar and left with fresh ideas.
